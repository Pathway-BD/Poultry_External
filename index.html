Bangladesh Livestock Heat Stress Calendar · Pathway Intermediates Bangladesh

Bangladesh's climate creates high heat stress risk from April through October, with peak danger months typically running May–September when both temperature and humidity reach critical THI levels for broiler, layer, and dairy operations.

Farms should begin STREX supplementation before THI levels cross the mild-stress threshold — proactive management significantly outperforms reactive treatment in maintaining productivity through summer.

THI Reference Guide

Understanding the Temperature Humidity Index.

THI combines ambient temperature and relative humidity into a single index that reflects the true thermal burden on livestock. A THI above 72 signals the start of heat stress for most commercial poultry species; dairy cattle show effects from THI 68 upward.
